What is Risk of Rain 2

How To

You start the game by picking a survivor, then you're dropped onto the planet. You spend your time in stages where you kill enemies to gain gold in order to buy chests and other interactables to buy items. You can progress to the next stage by activating the teleporter, spawning in a boss and many enemies, you must kill the boss and stay in the teleporter zone for enough time to charge it and escape. At stage 5 you have the option to proceed to the final boss or loop through five more stages, you can loop as many times as needed. Will you kill the final boss and escape the dying moon, or will you find other ways to finish your journey on this infested planet?
